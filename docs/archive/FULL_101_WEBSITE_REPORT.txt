================================================================================
                         1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

ImportationDotCA Inc. is a fully static, production-ready wholesale import/export
website built on Next.js 14, optimized for Hostinger shared hosting. The site
features a clean, professional dark theme with edge-to-edge layouts, responsive
design, and optimized performance for static hosting.

BUSINESS FOCUS:
- Wholesale import/export of décor products
- Confectionery (chocolate & candy)
- Hospitality goods
- Serving Canada & USA markets

SITE STYLE:
- Modern dark theme (slate-950 background)
- Professional flat design (no rounded corners)
- Edge-to-edge full-width sections
- No hover zoom effects or animations
- Clean, corporate aesthetic
